# Idea Screening

Use `templates/IDEA_BRIEF.md` as the required shape.

Screen the problem before the solution. Treat every app idea as a hypothesis about a repeated user struggle, a reachable buyer, and a small paid or behavior-changing outcome.

Evaluate:

- Problem intensity
- Frequency
- Reachable initial user segment
- Buyer and willingness to pay
- MVP speed
- Differentiation
- Regulatory, privacy, security, and operational risk

Evidence standards:

- Prefer recent user interviews, real workflow artifacts, search/forum signals, competitor behavior, and payment or time-saving evidence over opinions.
- Separate the user, buyer, approver, and operator when they are not the same person.
- Identify the current workaround and why it is painful, expensive, slow, risky, or embarrassing.
- Check whether a non-app solution, content, service, spreadsheet, or concierge workflow would validate the same need faster.
- Do not move to PRD until the next evidence gap is explicit and testable within 7 to 14 days.

Output:

- Score assumptions
- Decision: `ship`, `pivot`, `kill`, or `research_more`
- Top three missing evidence items
- First manual or concierge experiment
- Kill criteria
- Suggested next action
