# Idea Screening

Use `templates/IDEA_BRIEF.md` as the required shape.

Evaluate:

- Problem intensity
- Frequency
- Reachable initial user segment
- Buyer and willingness to pay
- MVP speed
- Differentiation
- Regulatory, privacy, security, and operational risk

Output:

- Score assumptions
- Decision: `ship`, `pivot`, `kill`, or `research_more`
- Top three missing evidence items
- Suggested next action
