# Market Research

Use current sources for unstable facts.

Required output:

- User segment
- Competitor map
- Existing alternatives and workarounds
- Market timing
- Legal, privacy, financial, medical, or operational constraints
- Sources used
- Open questions

Do not present unsourced claims as facts.
